Component 02

AI Prediction Engine

Trained on 15 years of phenology records for 40+ indigenous Italian grape cultivars. Predicts harvest windows 14 days ahead with 87% accuracy.

📲

Zero-friction onboarding

Scan QR on VineNode → WhatsApp opens automatically → confirm cultivar and target Brix → live within 15 minutes. No account creation, no app store visit.

🌅

Daily 7AM digest

Overnight soil status, frost risk, disease pressure, harvest forecast, irrigation recommendation — plain Italian, auto-generated by NLG pipeline.

🔊

Regional-dialect voice alerts

Urgent events (frost, severe disease) trigger 30-second audio messages in the producer's own dialect: Barese, Napoletano, or Siciliano.

🛰️

Satellite parcel images

Sentinel-2 false-colour stress maps sent directly in chat. Colour-coded by NDVI anomaly. Updated every 5 days.

🤝

Cooperative data pool

Anonymised microclimate data shared with neighbouring estates. Each new producer improves accuracy for the entire region.
