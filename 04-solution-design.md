# Step D — Solution Design

---

## Solution Name: **VineIQ**

### Tagline
*Precision viticulture for the world's smallest great wine estates.*

---

## What It Is

VineIQ is an **AI-powered precision viticulture platform** purpose-built for small Italian wine producers. It combines:

1. **A low-cost IoT sensor kit** (soil moisture, temperature, humidity, leaf wetness) deployable across a 5-ha vineyard for under €300 — solar-powered, LoRaWAN connected, maintenance-free for 3 years
2. **A satellite + drone imagery layer** using Sentinel-2 and Planet Labs data, processed per-parcel to generate NDVI, chlorophyll stress, and canopy temperature maps at 5m resolution
3. **A varietal-specific AI model** trained on 15 years of phenology records for 40+ indigenous Italian grape cultivars — predicting optimal harvest windows 14 days ahead with 87% accuracy
4. **A WhatsApp-first mobile interface** — because that is where Italian small producers already live. No app to download. No dashboard to learn. Alerts and recommendations arrive as voice notes and images in their existing chat.
5. **A cooperative data-pooling layer** — producers share anonymised microclimate data with neighbouring estates, improving model accuracy for everyone while maintaining competitive privacy

## Provider

**VineIQ SRL** — an Italian-registered deeptech startup headquartered in Matera (Basilicata), with R&D partnerships with:
- **CMCC Foundation** (Centro Euro-Mediterraneo sui Cambiamenti Climatici) for climate modelling
- **CREA** (National Research Centre for Agriculture and Economics) for 15-year varietal phenology dataset
- **Coldiretti** (Italy's largest farmers' association) as distribution and trust partner

**Legal form:** SRL (Società a Responsabilità Limitata), targeting B-Corp certification within 24 months.
