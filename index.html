"The tools that could save my harvest were designed for someone ten times my size."

Southern Italy's artisan wine producers — the custodians of Aglianico, Nero d'Avola, Fiano, and Greco — are losing their harvests to irregular heat spikes, compressed ripening windows, and unpredictable frosts. Every existing precision agriculture platform is priced and designed for industrial operators managing hundreds of hectares. The small producer is invisible to the market. VineIQ was built to change that.

How It Works

From order to intelligence
in under 48 hours.

1

Order your VineNode kit

Three sensor nodes delivered to your door. Your estate's cadastral parcel data is loaded automatically. Your cultivars are pre-configured.

2

Install in 30 minutes

Push each stake into the ground, angle the solar panel south, scan the QR code. VineIQ confirms connection via WhatsApp within minutes.

3

Receive daily intelligence

Every morning at 7AM: soil status, harvest forecast, disease risk, irrigation recommendation — in plain Italian, in WhatsApp.

4

Harvest with confidence

When the window opens, VineIQ tells you — 14 days ahead. Your neighbours in the cooperative data pool see their accuracy improve too.
